Top three activities to do at Verona

Attend a concert in the Arena

The Verona Arena is a Roman amphitheater located in the historic center of Verona, It is one of the large buildings that characterized Roman architecture and one of the ancient amphitheaters that has come down to us with the best degree of conservation.

Visit Castelvecchio

Castelvecchio is a medieval castel built in the historic centre of Verona, it is the most important militar monument of the Scaligera lordship.

Visit San Zeno Maggiore

it is an important place of Catholic worship located in the heart of the San Zeno neighborhood in Verona; it is one of the masterpieces of medieval architecture.
